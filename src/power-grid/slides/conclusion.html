Seeing the System

This explainer simplified a system that is, in reality, deeply complex.

We showed one way prices form — the merit order — but not differentiated demand pricing, long-term contracts, capacity markets, or regulation. We showed how traders respond to stress, not every way trading can fail.

What matters most for your bill is not any single actor, but the structure of the grid itself: the generation mix, the transmission constraints, and how flexible the system is under stress.

You now know enough to read headlines, price spikes, and political arguments with better questions in mind.
